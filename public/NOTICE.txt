Strudel Showcase

This site is a showcase that uses Strudel-compatible source files as creative
input. It is not a Strudel fork, extension, or official Strudel project.

The browser player imports @strudel/web and @strudel/soundfonts, which are
distributed under AGPL-3.0-or-later.

Strudel repository: https://codeberg.org/uzu/strudel
Strudel website: https://strudel.cc/

The musical works and Strudel source files under public/tracks are separately
licensed. See /tracks/LICENSE.txt in the deployed site.
